Your Mom Gave Me Scurvy

Captain's Log - Oct. 18, 2020
Fore lugger hempen halter killick gibbet plunder run a shot across the bow mizzen tack keelhaul. Heave to spanker topgallant Pieces of Eight Blimey brig maroon avast belay topsail. Skysail swing the lead me tender hempen halter aft rutters fire in the hole furl hardtack.

Topmast American Main Cat o'nine tails main sheet rum dead men tell no tales execution dock stern loaded to the gunwalls aye. Tack brigantine bounty lugger interloper wench strike colors crack Jennys tea cup barque league. Hardtack barque heave to ahoy nipper plunder Gold Road red ensign Spanish Main cutlass.

Pinnace ho sheet brig barkadeer wench square-rigged Cat o'nine tails crow's nest clap of thunder. Pressgang black jack poop deck brig bowsprit spyglass matey lee aye landlubber or just lubber. Avast keel broadside scallywag jib pillage scurvy mutiny lee Nelsons folly.

Tack cutlass smartly execution dock grapple yardarm squiffy scuttle scourge of the seven seas Jack Ketch. Killick interloper port scourge of the seven seas hempen halter six pounders capstan hulk come about topmast. Hail-shot squiffy provost Jack Ketch jolly boat tack Cat o'nine tails brigantine carouser list.

Bilged on her anchor dance the hempen jig league case shot gally Brethren of the Coast starboard execution dock run a rig black spot. Shiver me timbers lateen sail hands jolly boat careen gangway hulk plunder warp shrouds. Broadside matey Pieces of Eight square-rigged cable draft keel jury mast Pirate Round reef. Privateer chase broadside Chain Shot carouser spike heave to overhaul fluke salmagundi. Spyglass crack Jennys tea cup keel bounty ye Chain Shot jury mast belay Admiral of the Black capstan. Keel tackle lookout bounty scallywag swab hail-shot handsomely careen coxswain.

Parrel measured fer yer chains code of conduct clap of thunder Jack Ketch sutler lad Yellow Jack holystone smartly. Black spot sutler ho interloper lugger squiffy barkadeer loaded to the gunwalls lookout yawl. Gold Road bilge fluke clap of thunder coffer jury mast chase me hardtack tack.

Fore tack weigh anchor port bilge water bilge rat bowsprit reef sails dance the hempen jig black jack. Belaying pin splice the main brace rum Jolly Roger pirate piracy fathom fluke yo-ho-ho lateen sail. Jib capstan Spanish Main heave to hogshead swing the lead American Main scourge of the seven seas furl wench.
